Shipping & Delivery

Each collar is handmade before shipping, so delivery includes both production time and carrier transit time.

Production Time

Most custom collar orders are made within 1 to 3 weeks depending on the complexity of the design and current order queue.

Shipping Time

Once completed, orders are packaged carefully and shipped with tracking when available.

Custom Orders

Highly detailed patterns, special color requests, or wider collar builds may require additional time.

Delivery Notice

Chaos Collared is not responsible for carrier delays after a package has been handed off for shipment.
