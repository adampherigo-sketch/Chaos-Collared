Sizing Guide

Every Chaos Collared piece is handmade. Proper measurements help ensure comfort, safety, and the perfect fit for your custom collar.

How To Measure

  1. Use a soft measuring tape.
  2. Wrap it around the base of your neck.
  3. Measure snug, not tight.
  4. Add comfort space depending on your fit preference.

Fit Styles

  • Snug: Close fitted appearance.
  • Standard: Comfortable everyday wear.
  • Loose: Relaxed drape and movement.

Recommended Sizes

12" Very Tight Fit
14" Snug Fit
16" Standard Fit
18" Relaxed Fit
20"+ Loose / Layered Fit
