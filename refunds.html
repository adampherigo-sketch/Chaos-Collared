Custom order policy

Refunds & Returns

Because every Chaos Collared piece is handmade and customized, refunds and returns are handled carefully and fairly.

Custom Orders

Custom collars are made specifically for each customer. Because of this, returns may not be accepted once production has started.

Cancellations

Cancellations may be requested before production begins. Once materials are prepared or work has started, cancellation options may be limited.

Damaged Items

If your collar arrives damaged, contact us as soon as possible with photos so the issue can be reviewed.

Fit Issues

Please use the sizing guide before ordering. Custom sizing errors may not qualify for a refund, but adjustments may be discussed.
