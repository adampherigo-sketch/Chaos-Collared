About Chaos Collared

Handmade chainmail collars built for people who want something personal, bold, and beautifully different.

Built Link By Link

Chaos Collared is about turning metal, color, and personality into wearable art. Every collar starts with individual links and becomes something custom through pattern, length, color, and intention.

Whether you want something sleek and silver, electric blue, dark and industrial, or fully chaotic, each piece is designed to feel like it belongs to you.

The Style

The look blends chainmail craft, cyber energy, metal shine, and a little bit of mischief. It is bold without being boring and custom without feeling mass-produced.

The Mission

To create custom collars that let people express identity, style, confidence, and their own kind of chaos.
